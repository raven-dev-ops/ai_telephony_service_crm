ISO 27001 Partner Selection
===========================

Purpose
-------
Define a consistent, auditable process for selecting an ISO 27001 certification partner.

Scope
-----
Applies to external ISO 27001 certification bodies and consulting partners engaged for readiness
assessments, audits, or certification support.

Selection criteria
------------------
- ISO 27001 accreditation and auditor credentials (lead auditor experience).
- Proven experience with SaaS and cloud-native environments (Cloud Run, GCP IAM, GitHub Actions).
- Ability to align audit scope with `docs/ISMS/ISMS_SCOPE.md` and deliver a clear audit plan.
- Evidence requirements: sample audit report, control mapping approach, and evidence expectations.
- Timeline compatibility for target audit window (Q1 2026).
- Cost model and transparency (fixed fee vs. hourly, travel policy).
- References from similar clients and ability to operate in our regions/time zones.

Evaluation process
------------------
1) Build shortlist (3-5 partners).
2) Collect RFI responses (scope, timeline, deliverables, pricing).
3) Score using the rubric below.
4) Hold interviews and validate references.
5) Select partner and record decision in a GitHub issue.

Scoring rubric
--------------
| Category | Weight | Notes | Score (1-5) |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| ISO 27001 accreditation and auditor depth | 30% | Accreditation, lead auditor credentials, audit team size | |
| SaaS/GCP experience | 20% | Prior audits for cloud-native stacks | |
| Audit approach and deliverables | 20% | Clarity of audit plan, evidence expectations, sample report | |
| Timeline fit | 15% | Availability for Q1 2026 audit window | |
| Cost and contractual terms | 15% | Transparent fees, travel policy, renewal terms | |

Evidence to collect
-------------------
- Partner accreditation certificate or registry entry.
- Sample report outline (sanitized).
- Proposed audit plan and timeline.
- Pricing proposal and contract terms.
- References and contact summaries.
